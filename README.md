Description
========
Snakedowska is an educational and engaging video game that blends the classic mechanics of Snake with an homage to the chemistry and achievements of Maria Sk≈Çodowska Curie. Players control a "snake" that grows as it collects elements, isotopes, and compounds, with gameplay designed to teach and reinforce knowledge about radioactivity and chemical reactions. Ideal for students, educators, and chemistry enthusiasts, Snakedowska brings the wonders of chemistry to life through interactive and strategic gameplay.
